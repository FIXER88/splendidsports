Splendides! Welcome to our Splendid Sports official website. Our organisation is a registered Non-Profit Organisation that I'm very proud to be part of. The organisation was registered 2021 November and I became the first Chairperson and founder of Splendid Sports.

The idea of establishing such an organisation was to expose young african players especially living in places where variety of sporting codes are rarely visible. The name "Splendid" means something good, something excellent, and that's our vision as the organisation to NOT compromising the quality, excitement and joy of sport.

With that being said Splendies fam welcome to our website and Splendid coaches cannot wait to get to know you so am I!
