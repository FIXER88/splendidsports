Who we are?

Splendid Sports is a youth sport organisation in Mpumalanga province forcused on uplifting youth through sport.
We put our selves at the forfront of sport revolution, our vision is to see a black child in a rural area having the same quality of sport like the kid in the cities.

South African youth have faced education discrimination in the past and decided to protest against Bantu Education in 1976.
Youth of today, have limited choice of sport to play. Is either soccer for boys and netball for girls. How can black youth compete in this unequal sporting world?
